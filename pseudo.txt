AI-Advice to keep in mind...

    Your portfolio website itself is your most important project. It should be clean, fast, and responsive across all devices. 
    What to include:

    About Me page: Introduce yourself, your professional background, and why you are passionate about web development.

    Skill section: Go beyond listing technologies; provide context on how you have used them.

    Project showcase: Display your work with direct links to live demos and source code on GitHub. Include details on your specific contributions and the technologies used.

    Contact form: Provide a way for potential employers or clients to get in touch.

    Downloadable resume: Offer an easily accessible link to your resume.
